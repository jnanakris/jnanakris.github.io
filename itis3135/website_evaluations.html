Website Evaluations

Facebook Marketplace

Facebook Marketplace is an online platform for buying and selling items within local communities, integrated within the Facebook ecosystem.

  • Contrast:Clear typography and well-defined sections improve readability.
  • Repetition:Consistent layout and design elements across all pages ensure familiarity.
  • Alignment:Properly structured grid layout enhances organization.
  • Proximity:Items and categories are logically grouped, making browsing easy.

Craigslist

Craigslist is a classified ads website that allows users to post and browse listings for jobs, housing, goods, and services.

  • Contrast:Minimal design with a lack of visual hierarchy, making it harder to navigate.
  • Repetition:Consistent in its barebones aesthetic but lacks modern usability improvements.
  • Alignment:Poorly structured layout with inconsistent spacing.
  • Proximity:Cluttered sections and lack of visual separation reduce usability.
